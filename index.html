Our Story

Guitarras Mexican Grill

We have spent many years growing with the communty of Macon since as far back as 2010. Each year we try to put out as much love as we receive and serve our food with smiles. As we venture forward from our humble beginnings, we want to thank the community for supporting our dream and keeping the Guitarras dream alive. As we venture into new locations, you can bet we will bring the same love the community has supported us with since the beginning!
