About Us

The Resendiz Family

The Resendiz family extends a warm thank you for joining us today. Our menu reflects our noble commitment to providing you with a taste of home, using only the freshest ingredients and our utmost dedication. Growing up in Macon, Georgia, our family's love for our Mexican heritage and traditional cuisine inspired us to open a Mexican restaurant. We take pride in using the freshest local ingredients and time-honored recipes to become a beloved part of the Macon community, bringing people together to celebrate our shared cultural heritage.
